Who We Are

Jufresh Natural Fresh Fruit Juice is a growing agro-processing and green innovation company based in Nigeria. We believe that agriculture, health, and environmental sustainability can work together to create economic opportunities.

We are a youth-driven enterprise dedicated to improving public health and environmental sustainability through innovation.

Mission

To provide healthy, natural beverages while transforming agricultural waste into sustainable energy solutions.

Vision

To become a leading African brand in natural beverages and green energy innovation.

We convert agricultural waste into clean, affordable cooking fuel. This reduces deforestation, lowers household cooking costs, and minimizes environmental pollution.

  • Smokeless and odorless
